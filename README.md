# 🔥 Just Enough Fire (JEF)

Ever got annoyed by the massive fire texture blocking your entire screen while you are just waiting for it to go out? **Just Enough Fire (JEF)** is a lightweight Quality of Life (QoL) mod that fixes exactly this problem.

Instead of staring at a static fire overlay, JEF dynamically adjusts the fire on your screen to reflect how much time you have left before you stop burning.

### Features
* **Dynamic Fire Height:** The fire overlay slowly slides down your screen as your fire timer runs out. You will instantly know when you are about to stop burning.
* **Smooth Fading:** The opacity (alpha) of the fire smoothly decreases alongside its height, giving a satisfying "burning out" effect.
* **Client-Side Only:** This mod only modifies your screen rendering. You can use it on **any vanilla multiplayer server** without the server needing to have it installed!

### Compatibility
* **Mod Loader:** Fabric
* **Minecraft Version:** 26.1 (and compatible minor updates)
* No additional dependencies required (other than the standard Fabric API).

### Why "Just Enough"?
Because it leaves *just enough* fire on your screen to let you know you are burning, without ruining your gameplay and visibility during intense combat or exploration!
